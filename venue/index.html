Conference Venue

ENEOSホール (ENEOS Hall)

The conference will be held at ENEOS Hall, located in the RCAST Building 3-South on the University of Tokyo's Komaba II campus. The campus is situated near the vibrant Shibuya City, offering convenient access to various transportation options and amenities.

Address: RCAST Building 3-South, 4 Chome-6-1 Komaba, Meguro City, Tokyo 153-0041
