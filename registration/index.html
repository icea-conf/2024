Join ACM SIGAPP

  • Professional (SIG Member yearly Rate: $15.00)

  • Student (SIG Member yearly Rate: $8.00)

  • ACM Member or ACM SigAPP member rates are only available to individuals who are ACM or ACM SigAPP members at the time of registration.

  • ACM Member or ACM SigAPP members must input their ACM member or ACM SigAPP member id at the time of registration and show their valid ACM or ACM SigAPP membership card at the Conference registration Desk.

  • The Student Registration is applicable to University/College Students only. Pre-Registered Students and those registering on site must show a current STUDENT ID upon checking in at the ACM ICEA 2024.

Registration Policy

  • Each Accepted Paper at ACM ICEA 2024 must have at least one author registered with the Author rate at the camera ready submission deadline of September 29.

  • Each Accepted Regular registration can cover 1 Regular Paper.

  • ACM Member or ACM SigAPP member rates are only available to individuals who are ACM or ACM SigAPP members at the time of registration.
